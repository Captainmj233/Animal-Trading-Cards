Elephant


Elephants are known for their remarkable memory! They can remember and recognize other individuals, even after many years of separation.

  • Scentific Name: Loxodonta africana
  • Average length : 6.4 to 7 meters
  • Habitat : Savannah, Forest

Elephants are majestic, intelligent mammals known for their large size, distinctive trunk, and impressive tusks. They are highly social animals, living in tight-knit family groups led by a matriarch. With their keen intelligence and remarkable memory, elephants display complex behaviors, including communication through a variety of vocalizations, body language, and even through seismic vibrations sensed through their feet.
